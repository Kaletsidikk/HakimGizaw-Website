Emergency Department

About Emergency Department

Our Emergency Department provides 24/7 care for patients with acute illnesses and injuries. Our team of emergency medicine physicians, nurses, and support staff are dedicated to delivering prompt and efficient care to those in need.

We offer a wide range of emergency services, including evaluation and treatment for chest pain, trauma, respiratory distress, and other life-threatening conditions.

Services Offered

  • Triage and assessment
  • Diagnostic testing (X-rays, CT scans, etc.)
  • Emergency medical treatment
  • Advanced cardiac life support
  • Emergency surgery
  • Intensive care unit (ICU) services
