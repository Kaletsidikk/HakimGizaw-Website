Orthopedics Department

About Orthopedics Department

Our Orthopedics Department provides comprehensive care for musculoskeletal conditions, including bone fractures, joint injuries, and degenerative diseases. Our team of orthopedic surgeons and specialists are dedicated to restoring mobility and improving the quality of life for our patients.

We offer advanced treatment options, including minimally invasive surgery, joint replacement, and rehabilitation services, tailored to meet the unique needs of each patient.

Services Offered

  • Fracture care
  • Joint replacement surgery
  • Arthroscopic surgery
  • Sports medicine
  • Orthopedic rehabilitation
